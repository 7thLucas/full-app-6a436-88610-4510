# autoMoM — Product Overview

## Product Identity
- **Name**: autoMoM
- **Tagline**: Turn every meeting into clear decisions and trackable action — automatically.
- **Category**: AI Productivity / Team Collaboration Tool
- **Positioning**: The only meeting tool that captures, organizes, and follows up on everything — with zero manual effort.

## Users & Personas
1. **Team Leads / Project Managers** — Run frequent meetings; responsible for tracking decisions and action items across the team. Need clarity, accountability, and a searchable record of what was agreed.
2. **Team Members / Contributors** — Attend meetings; need access to summaries and their assigned tasks without having to take notes.
3. **Executives / Stakeholders** — Review key decisions and outcomes without attending every meeting; need confident visibility without noise.

## The Problem
Manual note-taking during meetings is error-prone, distracting, and inconsistent. Key decisions get buried in dense notes or lost entirely. Action items are forgotten. Follow-up tasks fall through the cracks. Teams waste hours re-discussing things already decided. There is no single shared source of truth for what happened in a meeting.

## The Solution
An AI-powered meeting assistant that automatically captures, transcribes, and organizes everything discussed. Upload a recording or connect a live online meeting — the AI handles the rest: full transcript, concise summary, structured action items with owners, decisions log, and automated follow-up reminders. Nothing is forgotten. Everything is organized.

## Core Features
1. **Meeting Ingestion** — Upload audio/video recordings or connect online meeting platforms (Zoom, Meet, Teams)
2. **AI Transcription** — Automatic, accurate, speaker-aware transcription of meeting audio
3. **Smart Summaries** — Concise AI-generated summaries highlighting key points and context
4. **Action Item Extraction** — AI identifies tasks, assigns owners, and sets deadlines automatically
5. **Decision Tracking** — Captures and logs every decision made during meetings, tagged to the project
6. **Follow-up Tasks** — Converts action items into trackable tasks with deadlines
7. **Reminder System** — Automated reminders for pending action items via in-app notifications and email
8. **Project Organization** — Meetings organized by project for easy retrieval and context
9. **Team Collaboration** — Shared access to meeting notes, summaries, decisions, and tasks across the team

## Strategic Principles
- **Zero friction**: No manual note-taking; AI does the heavy lifting immediately after (or during) the meeting
- **Nothing forgotten**: Every decision and action item is always captured, organized, and surfaced
- **Team-first**: Collaboration and shared visibility are core, not add-ons
- **Organized by context**: Project-based organization mirrors how teams actually work
- **Reduce meeting fatigue**: People can be present in meetings instead of furiously typing notes

## MVP Scope
- Meeting recording upload (audio/video files)
- AI transcription, summarization, and action item extraction
- Decision and follow-up task tracking
- Project-based meeting organization
- Team collaboration (shared access, task assignments)
- Action item reminders (in-app + email notifications)

## Tone & Brand
- Professional, calm, trustworthy
- AI-forward but human-centered
- Focused on clarity and organization
- Appeals to productivity-minded professionals and modern teams
