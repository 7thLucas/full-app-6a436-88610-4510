# autoMoM — AI Meeting Assistant

## What It Is
autoMoM is a B2B SaaS AI meeting assistant that helps teams capture, organize, and act on everything that happens in meetings. It eliminates forgotten action items by automatically generating transcripts, summaries, action items, decisions, and follow-up tasks from meeting recordings.

## Core Problem
Action items and decisions get lost after meetings. Teams waste time re-discussing resolved topics and miss deadlines because no one tracked what was agreed upon.

## Target Users
- All teams and divisions within a company
- Team leads, project managers, and individual contributors
- Anyone who attends recurring or ad-hoc meetings

## Day-One Killer Feature
Upload a meeting recording → AI auto-generates action items, decisions, summary, and transcript. Instantly.

## Full Feature Scope
1. **Meeting Ingestion** — Upload audio/video recordings or connect to online meeting platforms
2. **AI Processing** — Auto-generate transcripts, summaries, action items, key decisions, and follow-up tasks
3. **Project Organization** — Group meetings by project for easy navigation and context
4. **Team Collaboration** — Share and collaborate on meeting notes, tag teammates on action items
5. **Action Item Reminders** — Track pending tasks with due dates and send reminders
